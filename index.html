Ola' Adebayo

Ola' adebayo currently lives in Hamburg. He holds a master degree in Mechanical Engineering and another master degree in Joint European Masters degree in Environmental Engineering Technology and Management. He is a self-taught programmer. Being a self-motivated person made it easy for him to learn how to code when financial constraints would not allow him to hire a programmer for the start-up Company he co-founded with a friend of his. Ola' is a certified Scrum Master and a tech enthisiast. He has a sponge like mind and always willing to learn new stuffs. He has proven interpersonal skills and ability to solve problems. Ola' has worked with JavaScript, SQL, Python, HTML, CSS, MongoDB, Java and GIS Tools. New challenges only motivate him further.

Surface is a Content Management System (CMS) application designed for the health industry with a focus on the developing countries. The application provides digital presence for health center facilities. It is designed in a way that the owner of the center with or without IT skills can easily manage the center’s web content. Multiple contributors can also create, edit, and publish a particular health center’s web content.

About

Olalekan currently works as a full-stack developer in a start-up company he co-founded a few years ago. He is looking for new challenges in a new working environment. The past few years have been spent developing a broad knowledge base with the aim of gaining a robust overview of the full spectrum of Application development. Ola’ now has experience with both web application and native android development. He believes that the next step for him is to push his skills to the next level and gain deeper understanding by joining a bigger thriving company with a community of developers to learn from and support.

As a tech enthusiast who has participated in the product development lifecycle of successfully launched applications, Ola' has experience in coding, reviewing program functionality, establishing system improvements, and testing for security precautions. Equally adept at developing software for desktop computers, cloud integrations, and mobile devices.

Ola' also has years of experience working with satellite data and geospatial technologies for applications in urban & spatial planning, utilities remote monitoring, water distribution network analysis, community waste water network design, solid waste collection design, energy distribution network analysis, among others
